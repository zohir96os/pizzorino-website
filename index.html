Our secret reciepes!

1.Classic Margherita Pizza:

Ingredients:
  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Fresh mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
Instructions:
  1. Roll out the pizza dough.
  2. Spread a thin layer of tomato sauce over the dough.
  3. Arrange slices of fresh mozzarella on top.
  4. Place fresh basil leaves evenly on the pizza.
  5. Drizzle olive oil over the pizza.
  1. Sprinkle salt and pepper to taste.
  2. Bake in a preheated oven according to the pizza dough instructions.
  3. Once out of the oven, let it cool slightly before slicing.

2. BBQ Chicken Pizza:

Ingredients:
  • Pizza dough
  • BBQ sauce
  • Cooked and shredded chicken
  • Red onion, thinly sliced
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh cilantro, chopped
Instructions:
  1. Roll out the pizza dough.
  2. Spread a layer of BBQ sauce on the dough.
  3. Distribute the shredded chicken and sliced red onion evenly.
  4. Top with mozzarella cheese.
  1. Bake until the crust is golden and the cheese is melted.
  2. Garnish with fresh cilantro before serving.

3. Vegetarian Pesto Pizza:

Ingredients:
  • Pizza dough
  • Pesto sauce
  • Cherry tomatoes, sliced
  • Spinach leaves
  • Red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • Feta cheese, crumbled
Instructions:
  1. Roll out the pizza dough.
  2. Spread a generous layer of pesto sauce on the dough.
  3. Arrange cherry tomato slices, spinach leaves, and red bell pepper on top.
  4. Sprinkle crumbled feta cheese over the vegetables.
  1. Bake until the crust is crispy and the cheese is bubbly.

4. Mediterranean Olive and Feta Pizza:

Ingredients:
  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce or olive tapenade
  • Kalamata olives, sliced
  • Artichoke hearts, chopped
  • Red onion, thinly sliced
  • Feta cheese, crumbled
Instructions:
  1. Roll out the pizza dough.
  2. Spread a layer of tomato sauce or olive tapenade on the dough.
  3. Scatter Kalamata olives, chopped artichoke hearts, and red onion slices.
  4. Sprinkle crumbled feta cheese over the toppings.
  5. Bake until the edges are golden and the cheese is melted.
